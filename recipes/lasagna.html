Ingredients:

  • Mozzarella
  • Basil
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Riccotta
  • Parmesan

Instructions:

  1. Turon on oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Cook noodles in 7 cups of boiling water
  3. Layer noodles, cheese, and tomato sauce, alternating each time in a 9 inch by 9 inch pan
  4. Bake for 40 minutes until cheese is browning.
  5. Cool, cut, and serve with basil garnish.
