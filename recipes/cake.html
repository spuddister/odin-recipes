Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Chocolate
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa powder

Instructions:

  1. Turon on oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Mix all ingredients in a bowl and beat for 2 minutes
  3. Pour into 9x9 inch cake pan
  4. Bake for 30 minutes until browning.
  5. Cool, cut, and serve with vanilla ice cream.
